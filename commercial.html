Commercial FAQ

Questions about commercial cleaning

Do you offer after-hours cleaning?

Yes. We offer evening, overnight, and weekend cleaning to minimize disruption to your business operations.

Can you clean medical or dental offices?

Yes. Our teams are trained in OSHA-compliant cleaning protocols and use hospital-grade disinfectants for medical environments.

How is commercial pricing determined?

Pricing is based on square footage, cleaning scope, frequency, and any specialty requirements. We provide a custom quote after a brief walkthrough and phone consultation.

Do you provide supplies and equipment?

Yes. We bring all cleaning supplies, equipment, and products. We can also accommodate special product requests.

Are your teams insured and bonded?

Yes. All team members are fully insured, bonded, and background-checked for your protection.

Can we get a trial cleaning before committing?

Absolutely. We offer a one-time trial clean so you can experience our quality before signing a recurring contract.
